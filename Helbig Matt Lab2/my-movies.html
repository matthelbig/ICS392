My Movies

I chose "Objectified" as my top movie because it explores people's relationships with objects. Everything around you was made or designed at some point by a person. The film is directed by Gary Hustwit. His other films also try to look at how people change things and vice versa. His films really make you look at what is around you and think about where things come from.
