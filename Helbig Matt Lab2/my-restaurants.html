My Restaurants

One of my favorite menue items at Humphry Slocombe is an ice cream flavor called: "Secret Breakfast". The item consits of a vanilla ice cream base flavored with bourbon and cornflakes. While it is located in San Francisco, it still one of the better frozen treats I have had in my life. Add another scoop of their Blue Bottle Coffee flavor and you can have breakfast for dessert.
